Featured Proverbs

تا مرد سخن نگفته باشد، عیب و هنرش نهفته باشد

Until a man speaks, his flaws and talents remain hidden.

A person's character is revealed through their words

هر چه بکاری همان را درو می‌کنی

Whatever you plant, you will harvest.

Your actions determine your results; if you work hard or do good, you will get positive outcomes.
